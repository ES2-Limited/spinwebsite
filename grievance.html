Grievance Redress Mechanism

The SPIN Project is committed to transparent and fair project implementation. If you have a concern, complaint, or grievance related to project activities in your community, you can submit it here and it will be reviewed by the project team.

All grievances are logged, tracked, and responded to. You can monitor the status of reported issues in the public tracker below.

Common types of grievances

  • Wrong Name
  • Increase in Farm Size
  • Decrease in Farm Size
  • Land Encroachment
  • Claimed / Under Payment
  • Absent During Revalidation
  • Representation Issues
  • Change of Ownership
  • No Valid Identification
  • Other

Have a Complaint?

Fill out the grievance form and the SPIN project team will review your concern and get back to you.

Your identity remains confidential in the public tracker.
